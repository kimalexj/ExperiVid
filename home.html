Directions
  1. Hit the "Begin Posing" button to start.
  2. With the image overlayed on your camera, stand back and pose as the character!
  3. Whenever you are ready, say "click" and a screenshot will be taken. (Alternatively you can press "Enter")
  4. If at any point the voice recognition feels like it's not reading, wait about 6 seconds *without background noise* and try the phrase again.
  5. When you believe your screenshot is appropriate, press the "Download" button.
