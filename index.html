Directions
  1. Hit the "Begin Posing" button to start.
  2. With the image overlayed on your camera, stand back and pose as the character!
  3. Whenever you are ready, say "Take" and a screenshot will be taken. (Alternatively you can press "Enter")
  4. When you believe your screenshot is appropriate, press the "Download" button or say "Done".
